Your phone is a radio.
Do you know who's listening?

Adversaries use fake cell towers, rogue WiFi, Bluetooth trackers, network hijacking, and silent pings to locate, track, and intercept personnel through the devices they carry every day. Most teams have zero visibility across any of these layers. The result: accepted risk by default.

Fake Base Stations

IMSI catchers masquerade as legitimate towers. Your phone connects automatically. Identity, location, and comms exposed.

Forced Downgrades

Attackers force your phone from 5G/LTE to 2G, stripping encryption and enabling real-time interception of voice and data.

Silent Tracking

Type-0 SMS pings silently probe your device. Your location is mapped, your presence confirmed. You never know.

Evil Twin WiFi

Rogue access points clone trusted networks. Your device auto-connects. All traffic intercepted through the attacker's gateway.

Bluetooth Stalking

AirTags, SmartTags, and BLE trackers planted on vehicles or in bags. Persistent physical tracking with no alert from iOS or Android.

Network Hijacking

VPN silent drops, DNS hijacking, and TLS MITM attacks redirect your traffic through hostile infrastructure. You think you're protected.

Technical Specifications

Production-ready architecture

Built on modern Android development practices with enterprise-grade reliability and security.

Android Requirements

Min SDK 26 (Android 8+) for core detection. Full detection requires SDK 29+ (Android 10). Advanced features need root + Qualcomm DIAG.

Database & Storage

Room database with migration support. OpenCelliD CSV import (per-country). Local SQLite storage with encrypted exports via AES-256-GCM.

Languages & Architecture

Kotlin primary language. C (NDK) for Qualcomm DIAG integration. MVVM architecture with Hilt dependency injection and Jetpack Compose UI.

Propagation Models

Okumura-Hata urban/suburban models. COST 231 for extended coverage. Log-distance path loss calculations for geolocation accuracy.

Tower Database Integration

OpenCelliD dataset with per-country CSV imports. 40M+ verified cell towers globally. Real-time verification of detected towers against known infrastructure.

Important Caveats

  • Edge Sentinel is an early warning tool, not a protection system. It detects threats -- it cannot prevent attacks.
  • It cannot detect passive eavesdropping. If an adversary is listening without transmitting, there is no signal to detect.
  • Detection confidence varies by threat type and sensor availability. Effectiveness against sophisticated state-level systems is limited.
  • This capability supplements -- it does not replace -- TSCM, comms discipline, or established force protection procedures.
  • Advanced cipher and protocol analysis requires a rooted device with Qualcomm chipset. Core features work without root.
